About Us

JAIPUR'S MOST SERIOUS GYM

Founded in 2018, Titan Fitness was built with one purpose: to create a space where serious athletes and determined beginners can train side by side under the same roof with zero compromise on quality.

Located in the heart of Jaipur, our 8,000 sq ft facility is equipped with the latest commercial gym equipment, private coaching bays, a dedicated cardio deck, and a clean, high-energy environment that breeds results.

  • 8,000 sq ft premium training facility
  • Certified personal trainers & nutritionists
  • Separate strength & cardio zones
  • Steam room & locker facilities
  • Free WiFi & air-conditioned throughout
  • Protein bar & supplement store on-site
